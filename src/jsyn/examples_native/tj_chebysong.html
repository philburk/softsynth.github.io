Chebyshev Song

Play a polyphonic song based on a Linear Congruential pseudo-random sequence generator. The instrument is a WaveShaper with a Chebyshev Polynomial wave-table which provides easily controlled harmonic content. Repeating patterns are generated by reseeding the pseudo-random number generator. The tuning is a pentatonic scale from a 12 tone equal tempered tuning. 12TET is often used in the music of Europe and North America.
