Wind

How it works:
This wind noise is created by feeding white noise into a low pass filter.  The cutoff frequency of the low pass filter is being modified by a slowly varying random ramp function.  Change the values below to hear their effect on the sound. The "Stage" control is used to trigger state changes in a circuit. The oscilloscope gives  you a plot of the amplitude of the wind circuit versus time.
