## Four Pole Lowpass Filter

The FilterFourPoles unit emulates the filters in the modular Moog synthesizers. You can listen to either the Moog filter or the biquad lowpass filter.

A band limited sawtooth oscillator is played through the filter.

Wait for CheerpJ to run the applet. <script>cheerpjInit();</script>
