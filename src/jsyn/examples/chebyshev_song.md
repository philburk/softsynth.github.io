## Chebyshev Song

Play a polyphonic song based on a Linear Congruential pseudo-random sequence generator. Repeating patterns are generated by reseeding the pseudo-random number generator with previous values. The tuning is a pentatonic scale from a 12 tone equal tempered tuning. The instrument uses a WaveShaper with a Chebyshev Polynomial wave-table.
