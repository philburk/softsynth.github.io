# Synthetic Bird Songs

# How it works:

Synthetic bird sounds are produced by randomly varying the depth and rate of modulation. It was inspired by listening to [Great Tailed Grackles](https://xeno-canto.org/species/Quiscalus-mexicanus) in Cozumel.
