### This section discusses Time and How to  Schedule Musical Events. The major points are:

*   Time is measured in Ticks.
*   Absolute time must be used to prevent timer drift.
*   Sound events like start(), stop(), set() and queue() can be scheduled at a specific time.
*   Events must be scheduled in advance to avoid glitches.
