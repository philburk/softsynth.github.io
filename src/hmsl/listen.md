# HMSL - Hierarchical Music Specification Language

## Listen to Some Pieces

### By Phil Burk

[SUBDIV](/hmsl/piece_subdiv/) was composed by gamelan composer and electronic music pioneer Phil Corner and programmed by Phil Burk.

[REL\_TUNING](/hmsl/piece_rel_tuning/) explores relative dynamic just intonation.

[XForms](https://soundcloud.com/phil-burk/xforms-c30-t09) transforms a theme. Hear on SoundCloud.

[Yuppies in the Jungle](https://soundcloud.com/phil-burk/yuppies-in-the-jungle) is a performance poem accompanied by HMSL generated DSP sound effects and music. Hear on SoundCloud.

[Splorp](https://soundcloud.com/phil-burk/splorp) is an interactive algorithmic instrument. Hear on SoundCloud.

Look for RelNet and Squiggle on [this page](/music/).

[String\_4tet\_A](/hmsl/piece_string_4tet/) is a string quartet written using the score entry system.
