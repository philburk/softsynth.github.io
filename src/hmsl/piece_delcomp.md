# Delicate Computations

Composed by Phil Corner, programmed by Phil Burk

The sound consists of precisely defined series of ascending and descending tones and sequences to evolve over time.

An article in Spanish describing the piece is [here](http://experimenta.biz/revistaexperimenta/archives/3906).
