Play a Sample with the Mouse

How it works:
This applet loads a sample into memory and display an outline in the box below. Click and drag the mouse over the picture to hear parts of the sample. Drag faster to play faster. A cursor shows what part is being played.
If you drag near the top of the picture, then the cursor will move faster. Drag near the bottom to move slower.
Select new samples from the menu.
