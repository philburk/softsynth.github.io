2011 - We haven't made many press releases lately. But we have been very busy. Our main activities lately include:

*   Updating ListenUp recorder.
*   Developing Pure Java JSyn.
*   Ongoing development for Sony PS3 under contract.
*   Development of next generation music standards with MIDI Manufacturers Association.
*   Developing standalone unit tests for PortAudio.
*   Converting this website from 1997 HTML style to modern PHP+CSS style.
